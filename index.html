Treasure Collector

Rules of the game

  • Collect treasures to increase your score.

  • Avoid obstacles which reduce your score and time by 10.

  • You have 200 seconds to complete all five levels.

  • Advance through levels by collecting all available treasures.

  • You get one powerup per level to add 20 seconds to timer.

  • The game ends after Level 5!

Game elements

Gold: 5 points
Silver: 3 points
Bronze: 1 point
Obstacle: -10 points
Poop: -10 points
powerup: +20 seconds
