Treasure Collector

Rules of the game

  • Collect treasures to increase your score

  • Avoid obstacles which reduce your score and time in hand

  • You have 200 seconds to complete all five levels

  • Advance through levels by collecting all available treasures

  • The game ends after Level 5!

Game elements

Gold: 5 points
Silver: 3 points
Bronze: 1 point
Obstacle: -10 points
Rocks: -10 points


SCORE: 0
